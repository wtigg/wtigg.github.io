Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

The short version

DayDraw is built so your creativity stays yours. All AI image generation happens entirely on your iPhone. Your prompts and the images you create never leave your device, and we never see them.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your prompts.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit the images you generate.
  • We do not require an account, email, or sign-in.
  • We do not include analytics or advertising trackers that profile you.

Where your data lives

Generated images and their details (prompt, style, model, date) are stored only on your device, inside the app. If you delete the app, that data is removed. You can save or share an image at any time through the standard iOS share sheet, which is fully under your control.

Model downloads

AI models are downloaded once from public model repositories so generation can run on your device. These requests fetch model files only. They do not carry your prompts, your images, or any personal information.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by Apple and by RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider. To manage your subscription and unlock Pro features, RevenueCat processes a random app-generated identifier and your purchase receipt. It does not receive your prompts or images. See Apple's and RevenueCat's privacy policies for details on how purchase data is handled.

Children

DayDraw is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date above.
