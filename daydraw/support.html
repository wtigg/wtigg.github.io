Support

How does DayDraw work offline?

DayDraw runs Stable Diffusion directly on your iPhone using the Neural Engine. The first time you use a model, you download it once over Wi-Fi. After that, generation works fully offline, even in airplane mode.

Why do I need to download a model first?

The AI models are large, so they are not bundled in the app. Tapping download fetches the recommended model in one step. Once it is installed, you can generate unlimited images with no further downloads.

Which iPhones are supported?

DayDraw needs iOS 17 or later. Standard models run on most recent iPhones. The largest models, like SDXL, need a device with more memory, such as an iPhone 15 Pro or newer. Models that are not supported on your device are clearly marked.

How long does a generation take?

It depends on your device and the model. Fast few-step models can produce an image in a few seconds. Larger models take longer but give higher detail.

What does Pro unlock?

Pro unlocks all premium models including SDXL, unlimited batch generation, upscaling, and every advanced control. New subscribers get a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime in your Apple subscription settings.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. This re-syncs your subscription with your Apple ID.

Are my images private?

Yes. Everything is generated and stored on your device. Your prompts and images never leave your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy.
